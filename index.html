What is ECF?

ECF stands for Equity Crowd Funding, is a new way of collecting money from people to fund a project. It was regulated by POJK No. 37 which is published on December 2018

What is DIVIDEN?

DIVIDEN is an ECF Platform. It will bring together the investor and investee, so they can choose to fund or to be funded
